Legal Adherence

E-Waste Management Rules

We operate in strict accordance with the Basel Convention on the control of transboundary movements of hazardous wastes. For our clients, this means a "no-landfill" guarantee and full chain-of-custody documentation.

Our downstream vendors are audited quarterly to ensure compliance doesn't stop at our gates.

Data Destruction & Liability

In the age of information, hardware disposal is a security risk. MRM Eco Solutions provides NIST 800-88 standard data wiping and physical shredding for all IT assets. We issue Certificates of Destruction that transfer environmental and data liability away from your company.

  • Zero-Waste-to-Landfill Certificate
  • Full Chain-of-Custody Logs
  • Verified Carbon Offset Credits
  • Data Destruction Liability Cover
