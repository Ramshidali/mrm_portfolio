Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023
Quick Links: Data Collection | Usage | Security

At MRM Eco Solutions, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle personal information collected through our website and waste management services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and physical address for waste collection.
  • Service Data: Waste types, collection frequency, and site location details.
  • Digital Data: IP address, browser type, and cookies via our website analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide and improve our eco-services, including:

  • Scheduling and performing waste collection and recycling.
  • Providing sustainability reports and diversion metrics.
  • Processing payments and sending service updates.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. For E-Waste clients, we follow NIST 800-88 standards for data destruction to ensure no sensitive information remains on hardware during the recycling process.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with verified downstream recycling partners and government regulatory bodies as required by environmental law.
