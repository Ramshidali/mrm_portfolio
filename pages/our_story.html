How it all began

MRM Eco Solutions wasn't born in a boardroom; it was born in the field. Our founders saw the massive gap between "disposal" and "recovery" and decided to bridge it with technology and empathy.

Our Mission

To eliminate the concept of waste by transforming every discarded resource into a new beginning for the planet.

Our Vision

To power a world where business growth and environmental health are perfectly synonymous.

Our Journey

2015

Founded MRM Eco Solutions with a primary focus on organic waste composting in local residential blocks.

2018

Expanded into industrial asset recovery and secured our first major partnership with a national manufacturing firm.

2021

Launched our IoT-based waste tracking platform, achieving 90%+ diversion rates for our corporate clients.

2024

Recognized as a global leader in circular economy solutions, with operations spanning three continents.

The Values We Live By
